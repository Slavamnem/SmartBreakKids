# User data - SmartBreak Kids

The app does not store user data on its own servers now. Document will be updated when server will be created.

We access your email and name only via Google OAuth for authentication and to handle support requests.

If you want to request deletion of any associated data or revoke access, you can:
- delete the app from your device
- revoke access in your Google Account settings

You can also contact us directly at: v.zelinsky96@gmail.com to request deletion or data-related actions.
